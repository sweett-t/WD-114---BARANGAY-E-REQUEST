What We Offer

Available Documents & Services

Request any of the following documents directly from your device — no need to visit the barangay hall.

Identity

Barangay Clearance

Certifies that you have no pending cases or complaints within the barangay. Commonly required for employment, travel abroad, bank transactions, and legal purposes.

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of residency
  • Accomplished request form
Processing: 1–2 business days
Identity

Barangay ID

An official barangay-issued identification card for residents. Accepted as a valid government ID for transactions requiring proof of identity and local residency.

  • 1x1 or 2x2 ID photo
  • Birth certificate or valid ID
  • Proof of residency (6 months minimum)
Processing: 2–3 business days
Residency

Certificate of Residency

Confirms that you are a current and legitimate resident of Barangay Lunzuran. Required for school enrollment, scholarship applications, and government benefit claims.

  • Valid ID with address
  • Utility bill or lease contract
  • Accomplished request form
Processing: 1 business day
Social

Certificate of Indigency

Attests that a resident belongs to a low-income household. Needed to access free medical services, legal aid, educational assistance, and government welfare programs.

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of income or employment status
  • Referral letter (if applicable)
Processing: 1–2 business days
Character

Certificate of Good Moral Character

Certifies that a resident is of good moral character and standing in the community. Required for job applications, scholarship grants, and school admissions.

  • Valid ID
  • Barangay Clearance (if not yet obtained)
  • Letter of purpose
Processing: 1 business day
Business

Barangay Business Clearance

Required for any business operating within the barangay. Ensures that your business is properly registered and compliant with local ordinances. Must be renewed every year.

  • DTI or SEC registration
  • Lease contract or proof of business location
  • Valid ID of business owner
Processing: 2–3 business days
Family

Certificate of Solo Parent

Issued under Republic Act 8972. Entitles solo parents to government benefits including flexible work arrangements, parental leave, and discounts on goods and services. Requires a barangay interview.

  • Birth certificate of child/children
  • Valid ID
  • Proof of solo parent status (death cert, court order, etc.)
Processing: 2–3 business days
Senior

Certificate of Senior Citizen

Issued to residents 60 years old and above. Grants access to senior citizen privileges including 20% discounts, priority lanes, and government pension assistance. Requires a barangay interview.

  • Birth certificate
  • Valid ID with age verification
  • 1x1 or 2x2 ID photo
Processing: 1–2 business days
Legal

Blotter & Complaint

File a formal incident report or lodge a complaint with the barangay. Covers disputes, minor offenses, disturbances, and other community concerns for mediation and resolution by barangay officials.

  • Valid ID of complainant
  • Written account of the incident
  • Names and addresses of persons involved
Processing: Filed on the same day
About the System

Built for Residents,
Designed for Ease

The Barangay E-Request System is a digital platform that lets residents request official documents without the need to physically visit the barangay hall.

Our goal is to reduce waiting time, eliminate unnecessary paperwork, and make government services more accessible to everyone in the community.

Fast Processing

Submit requests online and receive updates in real time.

Secure & Private

Your personal data is protected and handled with care.

Mobile Friendly

Works seamlessly on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop.

Track Your Requests

Monitor the status of your documents anytime, anywhere.

How It Works

Request a Document in 4 Easy Steps

No more long queues. Submit your request online and pick up your document once it's ready.

01

Create an Account

Register with your full name, address, and a valid ID. Your account will be used to track all your requests in one place.

02

Choose a Document

Browse from 9 available document types. Select the one you need and fill out the online request form with your details and purpose.

03

Wait for Approval

The barangay admin will review your request. You will be notified by SMS or email once your document is approved, rejected, or ready.

04

Pick Up Your Document

Visit the Barangay Hall during office hours to claim your document. Bring a valid ID and your tracking number for verification.

Processing Time: Most requests are processed within 1–3 business days. Urgent requests may be accommodated upon coordination with the barangay office.
